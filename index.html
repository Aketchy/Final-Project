About Us

EcoHope is a youth-led environmental group committed to building climate resilience in our community through tree planting, eco-education, and sustainable practices.

Our Projects

Tree Planting Drives

Mobilizing local youth to plant trees and restore degraded land across the community.

Clean-Up Campaigns

Organizing clean-ups in beaches, riversides, and public spaces to promote environmental care.

Climate Education

Hosting workshops and forums to raise awareness about climate change and sustainability.
